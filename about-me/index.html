About Me

My name is Coy (or Bag of Fish). I won’t list anything majorly personal here; I believe you should learn from me. If that deters you, then we probably wouldn’t get along.

Due to past experiences online, I can be abrasive and make assumptions about others when I notice traits I dislike. Just talk to me like you’d introduce yourself in real life, and you’re fine.

I’m a beginner artist, guitarist, and composer. I’m also into philosophy: I’d call myself an optimistic nihilist & antinatalist. My favorite philosophers are Emil Cioran and Ludwig Wittgenstein.

I’m a multifandom geek — toss me a few buzzwords or questions and I’ll probably know what you’re talking about. Just don’t bother with hypermasculine stuff like martial arts manhwas or seinen “aura” hype.

I like feminine terms, but still call me “he.” For example: “Ms/Queen/whatever Coy — wow, he’s so badass.” Just don’t say that unironically, that’s weird.

I’m a minor, but I’m fine with adults interacting with me if you aren’t a creep. That’s all I’ll say here. If you’re normal, I’m chill.
